All your devices. One seamless experience.

When you use a Mac, iPad, iPhone or Apple Watch, you’re able to do incredible things. And when you use them together, you can do so much more. Make and receive phone calls without picking up your iPhone. Use your iPad to extend the workspace of your Mac. Automatically unlock your Mac when you’re wearing your Apple Watch. And copy and paste images, video and text from your iPhone or iPad to your nearby Mac or vice versa.
